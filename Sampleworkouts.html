Sample Workout Plans

3-Day Workout Plan

  • Day 1: Full Body
    • Squats: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
    • Push-ups: 3 sets of 8-12 reps
    • Bent-over Rows: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
    • Plank: 3 sets of 20-30 seconds
  • Day 2: Cardio and Core
    • Jogging or brisk walk: 20-30 minutes
    • Bicycle Crunches: 3 sets of 15-20 reps
    • Russian Twists: 3 sets of 12-15 reps per side
  • Day 3: Upper Body and Lower Body
    • Dumbbell Shoulder Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
    • Lunges: 3 sets of 12 reps per leg
    • Pull-ups or Lat Pulldowns: 3 sets of 6-8 reps
    • Tricep Dips: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

4-Day Workout Plan

  • Day 1: Upper Body
    • Bench Press: 4 sets of 6-8 reps
    • Seated Rows: 4 sets of 8-10 reps
    • Bicep Curls: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
    • Plank: 3 sets of 30-45 seconds
  • Day 2: Lower Body
    • Deadlifts: 4 sets of 6-8 reps
    • Leg Press: 4 sets of 8-10 reps
    • Calf Raises: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
    • Hanging Leg Raises: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Day 3: Rest or Active Recovery
  • Day 4: Push and Pull
    • Overhead Press: 4 sets of 6-8 reps
    • Pull-ups or Chin-ups: 4 sets of max reps
    • Dumbbell Flyes: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
    • Plank: 3 sets of 30-45 seconds

5-Day Workout Plan

  • Day 1: Chest and Triceps
    • Bench Press: 4 sets of 6-8 reps
    • Incline Dumbbell Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
    • Tricep Dips: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
    • Cable Tricep Pushdowns: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Day 2: Back and Biceps
    • Deadlifts: 4 sets of 6-8 reps
    • Pull-ups or Lat Pulldowns: 4 sets of 8-10 reps
    • Barbell Rows: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
    • Barbell Bicep Curls: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Day 3: Shoulders and Abs
    • Overhead Press: 4 sets of 6-8 reps
    • Lateral Raises: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
    • Plank: 3 sets of 45-60 seconds
    • Hanging Leg Raises: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Day 4: Legs
    • Squats: 4 sets of 8-10 reps
    • Leg Press: 4 sets of 10-12 reps
    • Romanian Deadlifts: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
    • Calf Raises: 3 sets of 15-20 reps
  • Day 5: Cardio and Core
    • Running or Cycling: 30-40 minutes
    • Bicycle Crunches: 3 sets of 15-20 reps
    • Russian Twists: 3 sets of 12-15 reps per side
